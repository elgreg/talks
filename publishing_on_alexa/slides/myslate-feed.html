They grab this feed once every 15 minutes or so (there's no SLA)

What's interesting about this?

The streamUrl is the MP3 that Alexa will start playing. If we don't have that, she'll read the mainText in her own voice

We learned that the ID can't change... we just change the streamUrl, updateDate, mainText, and titleText

It's only one item. If you provide a list, Alexa will read all of them... and your Amazon rep will tell you if that's ok (hint: it's probably not).

This isn't really like a regular feed - they're going to read the whole thing if you give it to them. In our case, we just added an implied limit of 1 to this. So this URL http://my.slate.com/proxies/alexa/audio_flash_briefing/?limit=20 would show 20 of them... albeit with the same ID.
