Audio > Reading text

If you have the resources to produce your own Audio, Amazon will tell you how long or short they'd like you to make it. Hence why ours is called "90 Seconds with Slate" and not "71 minutes with the Culture Gabfest"

If you aren't producing custom audio for each flash briefing, as many publishers aren't, you can have you content read by Alexa from the feed - allowing for a much less costly production and, likely, more current content.
